Agile Project Delivery

How We Deliver Your Project

We follow a structured Agile methodology — short sprints, regular demos, and continuous client feedback. You're never in the dark about what's happening with your project.

Phase 1

Discovery & Scoping

We meet to understand your goals, audience, and constraints. A detailed scope document and project backlog are created before any work begins.

Day 1–2
Phase 2

UI/UX Design

Wireframes and high-fidelity mockups are shared for your approval. You request changes until the design is exactly right — before a single line of code is written.

Day 3–7
Phase 3

Agile Dev Sprints

Development runs in 1-week sprints. At the end of each sprint you get a live demo link, a progress report, and the chance to reprioritize the next sprint backlog.

Week 1–3
Phase 4

QA & Client Review

Full quality assurance testing across all devices and browsers. You do a final walkthrough, submit any last revision requests, and sign off before go-live.

Final Week
Phase 5

Launch & Support

We handle deployment, DNS, and go-live. Then stay on for 30 days of free post-launch support — fixing bugs and ensuring everything runs smoothly.

What a Sprint Looks Like

Each development sprint is a focused 1-week cycle. Here's how every sprint flows:

M

Monday — Sprint Planning

We review the backlog with you, confirm priorities, and commit to a deliverable for the week.

T

Tue–Thu — Active Development

Code, design, and test. You can message us anytime via WhatsApp or email for updates.

F

Friday — Sprint Demo & Feedback

Live demo link delivered. You review, approve, or request changes. Feedback shapes the next sprint.

Our Agile Commitments

Principles we never compromise on:

  • Weekly sprint demos — you always see progress
  • Backlog reprioritized with you every sprint
  • Scope changes accepted — no surprise fees
  • Direct communication — no account managers
  • Working software over lengthy documentation
  • On-time delivery or we work until it's done
